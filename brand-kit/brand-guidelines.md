# Haze Tech Solutions — Brand Kit

## Brand Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary Cyan | `#00CFFF` | Headlines, CTAs, accents, links |
| Secondary Orange | `#FF6B00` | Highlights, gradient endpoints |
| Dark Navy | `#040D1A` | Backgrounds, dark mode |
| Surface | `#071526` | Card backgrounds |
| Text Light | `#E8F4FF` | Primary text on dark |
| Text Muted | `#8BA8C4` | Secondary text |

## Gradients
- **Primary**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #00CFFF, #FF6B00)`
- **Cyan**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #00CFFF, #0088CC)`
- **Subtle**: `linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(0,207,255,0.1), rgba(255,107,0,0.05))`

## Typography
- **Display/Headlines**: Orbitron (700, 900) — uppercase, letter-spacing 0.05em
- **Body Text**: Plus Jakarta Sans (400, 500, 600) — clean, modern
- **Caption/Labels**: Plus Jakarta Sans 600, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.08em, 11-12px

## Logo
- Icon: Hexagonal "H" with circuit-board lines, cyan glow, orange accent dot
- Full: Icon + "HAZE TECH" (gradient) + "SOLUTIONS" (muted)
- Minimum size: 32px for icon, 120px for full logo
- Clear space: 1x icon height on all sides

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional but approachable** — we're experts, not robots
- **Action-oriented** — "Automate your growth" not "We offer automation services"
- **Confident** — state results, not possibilities
- **Concise** — short sentences, clear CTAs

## Tagline
**"Built to Scale. Wired to Win."**

## Post Template Types

### 1. Announcement
- Background: Dark navy with cyan border/glow
- Headline: Orbitron bold, white
- Body: Plus Jakarta Sans, muted
- CTA button: Cyan gradient

### 2. Value/Tip
- Background: Subtle gradient overlay on dark
- "TIP" or "DID YOU KNOW" label in orange
- Content in white, clean layout
- Logo watermark bottom-right

### 3. Testimonial
- Background: Glass card effect
- Quote in white, larger font
- Client name/title in cyan
- Star rating or metric highlight

### 4. CTA/Offer
- Bold gradient background (cyan to orange)
- Large headline in dark text
- Clear CTA button
- Urgency text ("Limited spots", "This week only")
